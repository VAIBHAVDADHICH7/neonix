FAQ

Everything you need to know about switching to solar in Jaipur.

Will solar work during cloud/rain?

+
Yes, panels work on light intensity. While generation drops during heavy clouds, your Net Metering connection ensures you use grid power and balance the credits later.

How much space do I need?

+
Typically, a 1kW system requires about 80-100 sq. ft. of shadow-free rooftop space. A standard 3kW home system needs roughly 300 sq. ft.

What is the maintenance cost?

+
Maintenance is minimal (cleaning panels). We offer a comprehensive AMC at just ₹160/kW which includes regular cleaning and system health checkups.
